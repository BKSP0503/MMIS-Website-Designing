Knowledge intensifications would optimize resources in information collection and management through standardizations, categorizations, and classifications


  • Every organization has a vision and mission with respect to which all the actions are taken.

  • MIS of any organization can be classified, categorized, and standardized in formula form as:

    • M (a t o {m}) = k [1];

    Where,


    • M = Manual management of function specific outputs

    • a= Administration data base (Accounting, Auditing)

    • t/p= Technical/Prime Data base (Project & program specific activities, monitoring and reporting (geographically located & traceable))

    • O= Operational data base (Business development, Marketing/business strategies, Human Resources, Legal matters)

    • m= Managerial database (MIS) (Compiling efforts of data to knowledge form for administrators)

    • k= Knowledge


  • MIS operations and its automations currently, would reveal that any service/product of any technology is only part of whole of organizational MIS. Thus, there is a huge gap for knowledge MIS for administrators to delegate roles and responsibilities towards organizational goals and objectives.

  • MMIS (Knowledge-based spatial MIS) and the outputs of MIS would address this ridiculously huge gap of knowledge required for administrators to administrate towards organizational goals and objectives.

  • Knowledge extracted from the data mining process would be the input for knowledge intensifications a new dimension in MIS that can be used exhaustively by the most whoever has geographical relations for their economics.

    • k Lo+Le (A T O {M}) = KI [2];

    Where,


    • K = output of traditional MIS

    • Lo = Location specific organization databases

    • Le = Location specific external/other databases of organizational interest

    • ATO = Function specific knowledge outputs

    • M = Automated or defined process mining of knowledge

    • KI = Knowledge Intensifications for decisions that could map goals of the project when customized
